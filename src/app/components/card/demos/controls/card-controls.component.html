Buttons or other controls can be added to the right side of the header by instantiating a <ng-kit-card-controls> component, or by adding either the ng-kit-card-controls or xmCardControls directive.

If you want to style the entire card-controls area, content can be transcluded using the ng-kit-card-controls-area or xmCardControlsArea directive. This will override an <ng-kit-card-controls> component and it will not display in the dom.
